Elon Musk's Cosmic Journey

1971-1995: The Beginning

Born in South Africa, Elon taught himself coding at age 12 and sold his first game.

"When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor."

1995: Zip2

Founded Zip2, sold to Compaq for $307 million in 1999.

"I think it is possible for ordinary people to choose to be extraordinary."

1999: X.com & PayPal

Co-founded X.com, which became PayPal, sold to eBay for $1.5 billion in 2002.

"Persistence is very important. You should not give up unless you are forced to give up."

2002: SpaceX

Founded SpaceX to revolutionize space travel and make life multiplanetary.

"I would like to die on Mars. Just not on impact."

2004: Tesla

Joined Tesla Motors, leading it to become a leader in electric vehicles.

"The first step is to establish that something is possible; then probability will occur."
